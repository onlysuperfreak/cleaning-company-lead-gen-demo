# Bronx Fresh Cleaning Co. Landing Page

Live demo: https://onlysuperfreak.github.io/cleaning-company-lead-gen-demo/

This is a portfolio demo for a cleaning company lead-generation landing page. The fictional business, Bronx Fresh Cleaning Co., represents a local cleaning company serving homeowners, renters, landlords, offices, and small businesses.

The project uses plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript only. There are no frameworks, build tools, or package dependencies.

## Business Problem

Local cleaning companies often pay for Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, and local SEO without having a clear way to see which marketing channels are producing quote requests.

This demo shows how a simple landing page can help a cleaning business capture leads, track where those leads came from, and understand which services people are most interested in.

## Features

- Responsive landing page for desktop, tablet, and mobile visitors
- Quote request form with required fields
- UTM campaign tracking for ad and marketing links
- `localStorage` lead capture for front-end demo purposes
- Business Owner Lead Dashboard
- Lead summaries by cleaning service, property type, and traffic source
- Most recent leads table
- Clear demo leads button
- Built-In Ad Tracking section with a test campaign link

## UTM Tracking Test

Use this URL to test campaign tracking:

https://onlysuperfreak.github.io/cleaning-company-lead-gen-demo/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=deep_cleaning

When a visitor opens that link and submits the quote form, the demo lead is saved with the campaign information from the URL. The dashboard can then show that the lead came from Google CPC traffic for the deep cleaning campaign.

## How This Could Be Expanded For A Real Client

For a real cleaning business, this landing page could be connected to:

- Email notifications for new quote requests
- Google Sheets for simple lead tracking
- A CRM such as HubSpot, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or GoHighLevel
- A backend database for permanent lead storage
- Google Ads campaign tracking
- Meta Ads tracking for Facebook and Instagram campaigns
- Call tracking to measure phone leads from ads
- Automated follow-up messages by email or SMS
- Conversion tracking for quote requests and booked jobs

## Project Notes

Bronx Fresh Cleaning Co. is not a real business. This page is a portfolio concept created to demonstrate landing page design, lead capture, UTM tracking, and a simple business owner dashboard for a local service company.
